Cooking at Altitude

When you move to a higher altitude, the boiling point of water drops, due to the lower pressure. This has a profound effect on cooking! Read on to discover the adaptations you'll need to make at high altitude.

Pressure vs. Altitude

The relationship between pressure and altitude is a question of gravity. Atmospheric particles of nitrogen, hydrogen, and oxygen are held to the Earth because of gravity. The farther from the center of the Earth you move, the fewer atmospheric particles you'll find. Move the altitude slider in the demo to check it out!
